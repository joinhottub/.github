### What is Hottub?

Most of the internet is built for broadcasting to crowds. **Hottub is built for the opposite** — small groups around the interests, activities, and events you love, where you get to know a few familiar faces online or in VR, then meet in real life near welcoming local places we call **Cabanas** (libraries, coffee shops, bookstores, parks, and local businesses).

Private by design. Host-led, not algorithm-led. No public trust scores, and no open DMs by default — connection grows through shared context, not random messages.
